How it works
Single-tap sets your center target. Set Front / Set Back then tap the green edges (or long-press the green) for edge yardages. Tapping a new target near the current one keeps your front/back; moving far away resets them. Plays-like folds in elevation, wind, temp, altitude & humidity.
